Payments Fundamentals

A comprehensive introduction to modern payments infrastructure. By the end of this course, you will understand how money moves through global payment networks — from the moment a customer swipes a card or initiates a bank transfer to the point where funds settle in the merchant or recipient's account.

This course is designed for operations staff, new joiners to FinTech companies, compliance teams, and developers building payment integrations who need a solid conceptual foundation before working with payment APIs or regulatory frameworks.
